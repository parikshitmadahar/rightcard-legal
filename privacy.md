# RightCard Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** December 19, 2025

RightCard is designed to work without accounts and without collecting personal information.

## Information We Collect
RightCard does **not** collect, store, or sell personal information.

- No account or login is required.
- We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment details.
- We do not collect precise location.
- We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

## Data Stored on Your Device
RightCard stores the following **locally on your device** to make the app work:

- The credit cards you select in “Manage Cards”
- Your last selected spend category (if enabled)
- Cached card data files downloaded from our public data source (to support offline use)

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

## Network Requests
RightCard may download updated public card data (e.g., earn multipliers and notes) from a public URL to keep recommendations current. These requests do not include personal information.

## Sharing of Data
We do not share user data with third parties because we do not collect user data.

## Children’s Privacy
RightCard is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
If we change this policy, we will update the effective date and publish the updated version at this same URL.
